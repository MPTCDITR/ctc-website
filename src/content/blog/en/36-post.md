The Community Tech Center (CTC) project, which will be established in high schools across Cambodia, aims to promote digital transformation in public educational institutions and encourage digital adoption within local communities. The project is implemented in collaboration with the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport and receives support from local administrations.

The Community Tech Center (CTC) has four main facilities: a Computer Lab, a Training Room, a Post Office, and a Public Hall, which includes an Internet room and an Internet café.

Currently, the Community Tech Center (CTC) initiative operates 21 centers nationwide, providing digital skills training and postal services to citizens within local communities.

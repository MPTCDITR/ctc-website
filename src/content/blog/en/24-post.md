Sisters of Code continue training to promote gender equality in Cambodia.

Takeo Province, On the afternoon of October 11, 2025, from 2:00 PM to 4:30 PM, a team of three from Sisters of Code conducted training on Lesson 9 about Wix Website at three community technology centers, including Sokhaean High School, Bun Rany Hun Sen High School, and Hun Sen High School 1 in Meithun. A total of 35 students participated.

This training program was organized with the aim of prioritizing support for female students to enhance gender equality through building digital technology capacity and contributing to driving digital innovation in Cambodia, aiming to accelerate the digital transformation skills in technology with sustainability and effectiveness.

Please be informed that CTC at public universities is the long-term vision of CTU on the development of the digital sector to serve modern education and develop digital human resources within the community, both in the public and private sectors, to establish a resilient and thriving digital ecosystem within communities.

For more information, please visit the official website of CTC at www.ctc.edu.kh to stay updated on the latest developments.

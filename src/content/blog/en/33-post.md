The Community Tech Center (CTC) serves as a facility that provides digital skills training to high school students, civil servants, entrepreneurs, farmers, and local community members. The center aims to enhance digital awareness and promote the effective use of digital tools to support daily livelihoods, as well as to facilitate access to public services through online platforms.

In addition, the CTC actively promotes digital human resource development, strengthens digital literacy, and contributes to reducing the digital divide, while simultaneously providing postal services to citizens within local communities.

At present, the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications (MPTC) has established and operationalized 21 Community Tech Centers nationwide. The Ministry continues to develop strategic plans for further expansion to enhance access to digital services and training across the country.

For more information, please visit the official CTC website at www.ctc.edu.kh
to follow the latest updates and developments.

The Community Tech Center (CTC) project, which will be established in all high schools across Cambodia, aims to promote digital technology adoption (Tech Adoption) in public educational institutions and local communities.

The Community Tech Center (CTC) has four main facilities: a Computer Lab, a Training Room, a Post Office, and a Public Hall, which includes an Internet room and an Internet café.

Currently, the Community Tech Center (CTC) initiative operates 21 centers nationwide, providing digital skills training and postal services to citizens within local communities.

The Ministry of Post and Telecommunications also plans to construct 10 additional Community Tech Centers (CTCs) in 2026 to promote inclusive, efficient, and effective digital transformation.
